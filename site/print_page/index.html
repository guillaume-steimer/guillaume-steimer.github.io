    About me

    I hold a PhD in applied mathematics from the University of Strasbourg at the Institut de Recherche Mathématique Avancée Mixed Research Unit 7501 from the CNRS. My thesis is entitled Model order reduction method for Hamiltonian dynamics using deep learning.

    Contact

    guillaume.steimer@math.unistra.fr

    guillaume.steimer@inria.fr

                     

    Domain & activity

    I am a former member of the MOCO and MACARON teams. My field of research is the model order reduction (MOR) of Hamiltonian systems.
