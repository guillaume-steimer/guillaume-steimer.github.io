# Domain & activity

I am a former member of the [MOCO](https://irma.math.unistra.fr/teams/moco.html) and [MACARON](https://www.inria.fr/en/macaron) teams. My field of research is the model order reduction (MOR) of Hamiltonian systems.

# CEMRACS summer school 2022

I participated in [CEMRACS 2022](http://smai.emath.fr/cemracs/cemracs22/index.html) on the theme "Transport in Physics, Biology, and Urban Traffic". I took part in a project titled "Model order reduction in velocity space and learning for the Vlasov-Maxwell equations" which resulted in a publication [doi:10.1051/proc/202477213](https://doi.org/10.1051/proc/202477213).
